Async Quiz App

Welcome to the Async Quiz App! Test your knowledge with multiple-choice quizzes or practice your math skills with our Math Riddle Solver.

How to Use
  • Go to the Join tab and enter your name to start the quiz.
  • Each quiz question has a timer. Select your answer before time runs out.
  • After finishing, your score is shown interactively. You can return to the home page.
  • Try the Math Riddle Solver tab for unlimited math practice!
Rules
  • Practice tests to improve your knowledge.
  • Each user can attempt unlimited quizzes.
  • Scores are saved anonymously.
About Math Riddle Solver
  • Choose from Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division, or Modulus modes.
  • Each riddle is generated automatically and instantly.
  • Type your answer and get instant feedback.
  • After each answer, a new riddle appears automatically.
  • This mode is for practice only.
